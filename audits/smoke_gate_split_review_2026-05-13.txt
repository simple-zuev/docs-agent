Smoke gate split review

Scope
- scripts/regression_exit_codes.sh
- scripts/regression_smoke_explain.sh
- assisted bounded launch contour

Confirmed findings
- regression_exit_codes.sh originally depended on bare python/PATH behavior
- exit-code regression also mixed:
  - bounded launch baseline checks
  - degraded document-access checks
  - helper/query checks
- this made regression_smoke_explain.sh unsuitable as a clean launch gate for assisted bounded mode
- runtime validation confirmed the usable bounded contour separately from degraded/helper routes

Decision
- split launch-blocking bounded checks from extended non-baseline checks
- keep bounded smoke deterministic and aligned with launch contour
- preserve visibility of extended checks as a separate script

Expected outcome
- regression_smoke_explain.sh blocks launch only on bounded baseline regressions
- degraded/helper regressions remain inspectable without redefining launch boundary
