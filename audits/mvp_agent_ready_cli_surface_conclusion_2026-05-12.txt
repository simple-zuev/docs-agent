Conclusion
- the application should be driven toward a minimum viable agent-ready CLI rather than broad full-surface hardening
- the recommended MVP surface consists of:
  - read / inspect foundation: status, repo-state, doctor, find-doc-id, find-doc-name, find-link
  - document access foundation: read-doc, get-file, read-doc-from-query
  - minimum mutation contour: artifact-state, doc-body-only

Recommended next step
- perform a bounded audit wave across the read / inspect foundation first
